# Predefined Output templates

Predefined Output templates cannot be edited, they are generated by Substance 3D Painter itself. Predefined Output templates appear at the top of the <b>Output template dropdown list </b>on the <b>Settings tab</b> of the <b>Export window</b>.

Below is a list of the non-editable presets available :

* 2D View
* Document channels + Normal + AO (No Alpha)
* Document channels + Normal + AO (With Alpha)
* Sketchfab
* Substance 3D Stager
* [USDz (Apple AR)](../../../../help/getting-started/export/export-presets/predefined-presets/usd-pbr-metal-roughness/usd-pbr-metal-roughness-preset.md)
* glTF PBR Metal Roughness
* glTF PBR Metal Roughness + Displacement texture (experimental)
