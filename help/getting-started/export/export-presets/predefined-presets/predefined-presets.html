Predefined Output templates

Predefined Output templates cannot be edited, they are generated by Substance 3D Painter itself. Predefined Output templates appear at the top of the Output template dropdown list on the Settings tab of the Export window.

Below is a list of the non-editable presets available :

  • 2D View
  • Document channels + Normal + AO (No Alpha)
  • Document channels + Normal + AO (With Alpha)
  • Sketchfab
  • Substance 3D Stager
  • USDz (Apple AR)
  • glTF PBR Metal Roughness
  • glTF PBR Metal Roughness + Displacement texture (experimental)
