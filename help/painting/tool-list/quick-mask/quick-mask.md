# Quick mask

Quick masking allows you to mask parts of your mesh you don't want to paint on.

* Hit **Y** to switch to quick mask edit mode, this will allow you to paint a temporary mask.
* Hit **U** again to switch back to your previous tool and paint across that mask.
* Hit **Y** to reset/disable the mask.
* Hit **I** to invert the mask while in quick mask edit mode.
