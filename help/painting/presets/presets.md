# Presets

A Preset is a saved configuration of a [Painting Tools](../../painting/tool-list/tool-list.md). They can be one of three different kind:

* **Brush Preset** : Saves the brush parameters (size, flow, jitter, stencil and alpha).
* **Material Preset** : Saves the material properties (individual channels and Substance files).
* **Tool Preset** : Saves both the Brush and Material properties as well as which type of tool was used (Paint, Smudge, Clone, etc).

Preset are saved on the disk within the [Assets](../../interface/assets/assets.md) folder. They can be copied across computers easily as they embed all the resources they use.

To manage your presets, see:

* [Creating and saving presets](https://helpx.adobe.com/substance-3d/unlisted/documentation/spdoc/creating-and-saving-a-preset-180191514.html)
* [Creating particles presets](../../painting/presets/creating-particles-pre/creating-particles-presets.md)
* [Photoshop Brush Presets (ABR)](../../painting/presets/photoshop-brush-presets/photoshop-brush-presets-abr.md)
