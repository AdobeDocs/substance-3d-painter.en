Presets

A Preset is a saved configuration of a Painting Tools. They can be one of three different kind:

  • Brush Preset : Saves the brush parameters (size, flow, jitter, stencil and alpha).
  • Material Preset : Saves the material properties (individual channels and Substance files).
  • Tool Preset : Saves both the Brush and Material properties as well as which type of tool was used (Paint, Smudge, Clone, etc).

Preset are saved on the disk within the Assets folder. They can be copied across computers easily as they embed all the resources they use.
